Dwarfs

Dwarfy leaps down as if struck by a stick,

Exclaiming, “Sweet Jesus, let’s set to work quick!


“When I’m not like a lion, I am just like a sheep –

While I stand around bleating, my posture is meek.


“Yet this ground feels like velvet,

and my heart, it can sing –


So while building our kingdom

I shall shed this old skin!


“Now as for your sister, let her teach what she can!

As our priestess she will show what’s concealed to a man!


"Like a mud-covered lily! Lo and behold!

Like she blossoms, so too will our hearts unfold!


“Our walls will be steady, and so will our core –

But wait, what was it we're striving for?”


We want to build a castle of sand that blows with the wind, just like the wind. That way we won’t be bound to the tricky laws of the earth.
I forget. Let’s take a break. We got a little caught up in our excitement, but I have a suspicion that our plans are futile.
We’re striving to build a pompous castle with everlasting barricades to intimidate our enemies. Those smug idiots.
We’re looking to erect a kingdom in the spirit of community, built upon peaceful soil by every helping hand we can find. This will create strong bonds and ensure optimal conditions for everyone’s unfolding.

Just a little more to go. Please choose your choice.
