Dragons

So that’s what you say; dragons ought to adapt?

That they better fly sweating while the new world is mapped?


So rush down through the forest, and then dropping your sword,

From a bush, you’ll call “Is this the New World Resort?


“I have come with good manners, and with pure intent:

Let’s reduce our emissions to help out a huge friend.”


What, dear friend, upon that day,

Do you think these friends will say?

"Nay is nay."
"Well, okay."
“We’ve heard of you. Heroic one day, depressed the next. Why should we trust you? We’re kind of comfortable here in our resort. The motto of this resort is: Shall new be as old! Pick up your sword and try to convince us otherwise!”
Some will resist or hesitate, but most will join me. They have a fresh vision after all and the collective spirit is strong. Besides, people who live behind forests have much veneration for dragons.
